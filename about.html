About me

I’m an aspiring data scientist who traded balance sheets for XG Boost models. With a foundation in accounting, I bring analytical rigor, attention to detail, and a relentless curiosity to every dataset I explore.

My journey began in July 2024 after a life-changing YouTube video sparked a simple question: “What if I learned Python for just one month?” I picked up Python Crash Course by Eric Matthes—and never looked back. Within three months, I was hooked on coding, and soon realized: Python was just the beginning.

Now, I’m diving deep into machine learning, SQL, and data visualization—bridging the gap between raw data and real-world decisions. Because to me, data isn’t just numbers; it’s a story waiting to be told.
